# UniProt

## Purpose
UniProt is a comprehensive, high-quality protein sequence and functional information database. Its goal is to provide a central resource where researchers can find reliable data about any protein.

## Information Available
- **Protein names** – official and alternative names  
- **Functions** – what the protein does in the cell  
- **Structures** – links to 3D structures (often from PDB)  
- **Biological roles** – pathways, interactions, and cellular locations  

## Why It Matters
Proteins are the workhorses of the cell, and understanding their function is key to understanding life. UniProt organizes this information so that scientists can quickly learn about a protein’s function, evolution, and relevance to disease.
