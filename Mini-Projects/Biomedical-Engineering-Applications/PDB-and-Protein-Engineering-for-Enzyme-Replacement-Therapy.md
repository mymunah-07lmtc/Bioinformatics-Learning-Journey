# Biomedical Engineering Applications

## PDB and Protein Engineering for Enzyme Replacement Therapy
- **BME problem**: Patients with genetic enzyme deficiencies (e.g., Gaucher disease – lack of glucocerebrosidase).
- **Bioinformatics role**:
  - **PDB** provides 3D structure of the normal enzyme (e.g., 1OGS for glucocerebrosidase).
  - Engineers identify exposed amino acids that trigger immune responses or cause rapid clearance.
  - Mutations are introduced (in silico) to improve stability, activity, or targeting.
- **BME product**:
  - **Velaglucerase alfa** – an engineered glucocerebrosidase for intravenous administration.
- **Skill development**: I want to learn molecular dynamics simulation (e.g., GROMACS) to predict effects of mutations before wet‑lab testing.
