# Protein of the week
## Week 3: Cas9 (CRISPR-associated protein 9)
- **Function**: RNA‑guided DNA endonuclease – cuts specific DNA sequences.
- **Source**: *Streptococcus pyogenes*.
- **Structure**: Multi‑domain protein with two nuclease lobes (HNH and RuvC).  
  *PDB ID: 4OO8* (bound to sgRNA and target DNA).
- **Databases used**:
  - **NCBI Gene ID**: 901394 (Cas9 from S. pyogenes).
  - **UniProt**: Q99ZW2 – includes domain annotations and active site residues.
- **BME relevance**: Foundation of CRISPR gene editing. Used in therapeutic applications – correcting mutations, engineering immune cells (CAR‑T), and creating disease models.
