# Protein of the Week

## Week 4: Human Insulin
- **Function**: Hormone that regulates blood glucose by promoting glucose uptake.
- **Structure**: Two polypeptide chains (A and B) connected by disulfide bonds.  
  *PDB ID: 3I40* (insulin hexamer, stored form).
- **Bioinformatics insights**:
  - **UniProt** (P01308) shows the preproinsulin signal peptide, proinsulin processing, and mature chains.
  - **BLAST** comparison across mammals revealed highly conserved regions – critical for binding to the insulin receptor.
  - **NCBI PubMed** search: “insulin analogs” – many engineered variants (e.g., rapid‑acting lispro).
- **BME application**: Design of improved insulin analogs, glucose‑responsive insulin delivery systems, and artificial pancreas devices.
